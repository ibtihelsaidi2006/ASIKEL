Travel Essentials

Frequently asked questions about our private services.

How far in advance should I book?

expand_more

For bespoke tours, we recommend booking at least 3-6 months in advance to secure the most exclusive luxury accommodations and specific private guides.

Can itineraries be adjusted during the trip?

expand_more

Yes, one of the primary benefits of a private tour is flexibility. Your dedicated concierge and guide can pivot the daily schedule based on your interests and local conditions.

What is the group size limit for private tours?

expand_more

Our private tours are typically designed for solo travelers, couples, or small groups of friends and family (up to 12 guests). For larger groups, we can arrange multi-vehicle convoys.

Are international flights included?

expand_more

We focus on ground arrangements and domestic connections. However, our travel consultants can assist in coordinating with your preferred international carriers or private jet services.

We strongly recommend comprehensive travel insurance for all our guests to ensure peace of mind during your Tunisian journey.

Most Western travelers enjoy visa-free entry for up to 90 days. Our specialists will provide specific guidance based on your nationality.
