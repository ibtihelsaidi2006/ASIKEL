Traveler's Essentials

When is the best time to visit?

Spring (March to May) and Autumn (September to November) offer the most pleasant temperatures for exploring both the Mediterranean coast and the Sahara dunes.

Can I customize my itinerary?

Absolutely. Asikel Voyages specializes in bespoke journeys. Every destination and experience can be tailored to your personal interests and pace.

Is travel insurance required?

While not mandatory for entry, we strongly recommend comprehensive travel insurance to cover unexpected changes during your adventure.

What should I pack for the Sahara?

Lightweight cotton clothing for the day and warm layers for the cool desert nights. Don't forget sturdy walking shoes.
