Our Commitment

Preserving the Essence of Tunisia

At Asikel Voyages, we believe luxury and sustainability are inseparable. Discover how we protect our heritage and landscapes through conscious travel.

Values that Define Us

eco

Évasion Consciente

Carbon-neutral explorations that respect the delicate balance of Tunisia's diverse ecosystems, from the Atlas mountains to the Mediterranean shores.

temple_hindu

Authenticité Locale

Direct support for Amazigh artisans and rural cooperatives, ensuring your visit translates into sustainable economic growth for host communities.

water_drop

Héritage Préservé

A rigorous plastic-free policy across all our journeys, coupled with water conservation techniques inspired by ancient Roman engineering.

Empowering Communities

Beyond Tourism, A Social Pact

Every Asikel voyage contributes a percentage of its proceeds to the Atlas Heritage Fund. We prioritize small, family-owned guesthouses and locally sourced organic produce, keeping the cultural fabric of Tunisia vibrant and resilient.

Our Impact Report 2025
flare

Zero-Waste Expeditions

We have eliminated 100% of single-use plastics from our safari camps and city excursions. Guests receive reusable artisan-crafted copper flasks upon arrival.

forest

Reforestation

For every traveler, we plant ten olive trees in the semi-arid regions of central Tunisia to combat desertification.
