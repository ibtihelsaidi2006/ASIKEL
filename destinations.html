The Journey

Trace the ancient paths of the Berbers through the heart of Southern Tunisia. This curated expedition is designed for the discerning voyager who seeks more than a view—you seek the soul of the landscape. From the surreal salt flats of Chott el Djerid to the troglodyte dwellings of Matmata, experience the raw beauty and sophisticated heritage of the Maghreb.

Daily Itinerary

Day 1

Arrival & Tozeur Oasis

Arrival in Tozeur, the city of bricks. Settle into a luxury traditional dar before a sunset walk through the historic palmeraie, guided by local conservationists.

Day 2

The Great Salt Lake

Cross the shimmering expanse of Chott el Djerid. Experience the mirages of the Sahara's largest salt pan before heading deep into the red dunes of Douz.

Day 3

Camel Trek & Star-Gazing

A slow-paced journey on camelback through the Grand Erg Oriental. Spend the night in a private desert camp under one of the clearest night skies on Earth.

Day 4

Matmata Dwellings

Venture into the lunar landscapes of Matmata. Dine with a local Berber family in their subterranean troglodyte home and learn the art of traditional weaving.
