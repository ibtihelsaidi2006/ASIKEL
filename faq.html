Planning Your Trip

The ideal windows for Tunisian exploration are Spring (March to May) and Autumn (September to November). During these periods, the Mediterranean breeze is gentle, and the Sahara temperatures are comfortable for both daytime treks and evening stargazing. Summer offers vibrant coastal life, while Winter is perfect for those seeking the quiet solitude of the desert dunes.

Visa requirements vary by nationality. Many Western nations enjoy visa-free entry for up to 90 days. We recommend checking with the nearest Tunisian consulate 6-8 weeks prior to departure. Asikel Voyages provides comprehensive documentation support for all our guests to ensure a smooth entry process.

Accommodations & Transport

Our signature Saharan camps redefine "glamping." You will reside in handcrafted Berber tents equipped with plush bedding, private en-suite facilities, and authentic local decor. Each evening features curated gourmet dining under the stars, accompanied by traditional music and the unparalleled silence of the desert.

All internal transport is managed via private, climate-controlled luxury SUVs (4x4) driven by our expert local guides. For specific segments, we also offer traditional camel caravans or private chartered regional flights to maximize your time spent exploring rather than commuting.

Sustainability & Ethics

Asikel Voyages is committed to a zero-single-use-plastic goal. Guests are provided with high-quality, insulated copper water flasks upon arrival, with filtered water stations available in all vehicles and camps. We strictly avoid pre-packaged items and work with local artisans who use traditional, biodegradable materials.

We operate on a "Community First" model. Over 80% of our supply chain—from guides to produce suppliers—is local. A portion of every booking fee goes directly into the Asikel Heritage Fund, which supports the restoration of ancient Berber granaries and funds education initiatives in remote Saharan villages.

Booking & Cancellation

We offer flexible cancellation terms. Cancellations made more than 60 days before departure receive a full refund minus a small administrative fee. For closer dates, we provide credit for future voyages or a partial refund. We highly recommend comprehensive travel insurance for all our guests.
